WE BELIEVE

At Road2Recovery, we believe you can overcome the toughest battles of your life against alcohol and drug abuse.

ALCOHOL

  • * Dependence on alcohol develops over time.However, a person can suffer from alcohol poisoning on his or her first drinking occasion, depending on the consumption level.

  • * Several million people engage in risky, binge drinking patterns that could lead to alcohol problems or persist a family history of alcoholism.

  • * Alcohol is the perfect solvent that dissolves marriages,families and careers causing serious health complications and emotional instability.

DRUGS

  • * In Canada, cocaine is the most common drug of injection users causing death due to overdoses.

  • * Cocaine is a highly addictive drug that produces intense feelings of euphoria, power and confidence. All of these side effects make the risk of abuse very high for even the occasional cocaine user.

  • * Toronto treatment centers report that cocaine is second only to alcohol as the most problematic substance requiring treatment.
