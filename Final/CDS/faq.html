We are here to clarify your queries.

Q1. I am new to this site. How do I register?
ANS. If you are new to Road2Recovery site and haven’t cosulted any doctors, you will need to register. Registration collects some details about you including your name, email address, phone number, type of addtiction etc. Ensure accuracy when entering these details as they are used to verify your record to send consultation updates (via email). To register,select the Manage Accounts button on the homepage. The sites Terms of Use will display, then after accepting ,the registration form needs to be completed and select ‘Sign up’
Q2. What should I do if I forget my password?
ANS. Select the Forgotten Password link on the login page, put in your email address and select search for your password to be emailed to you. If your email address is registered within the system an email will be sent with instructions on how to change your password. A detailed user guide to changing your password is available Forgotten password.
Q3. What are the differnt types of substance abuse do we deal with?
ANS. Road2Recovery deals with two type of addictions which is Alcohol Addictions and Drug specifically Cocaine Addiction. We help people in order to come over the addictions and lead a happy life.
Q4. What is the duration of the rehab process?
ANS. The duration of each rehab process varies. At Road2Recovery we analyze your level of addiction, previous medications (if any) and propose the duration accordingly.
Q5. Is rehab a depressing place?
ANS. Rehab is where patients go once their conditions improve to try to regain their independence. While it is not uncommon to see tears and signs of frustration on rehab, there is little in medicine so uplifting and inspiring as seeing someone who has overcome great hurdles achieve their goals!
